The FairyTale Farms

The Best Place to Live A Luxurious Life

Nurtured by the picturesque canvas of nature, The FairyTale Farms is your little slice of heaven on earth in this bustling yet serene corner of Bangalore. Imagine waking up to a symphony of birds chirping around, the rustling of leaves that sway coolly with the breeze, and the soft glimmer of a nearby lake reflecting morning sun rays. That your day just starting here but an experience, an escape, and maybe even your new favourite place to hit pause.

The FairyTale Farms Experience

The FairyTale Farms believes in two things which are Soak in Nature and perfect place to rewind. Every new legacy built has an expectation to live up to expectations of our ever smiling customers. The only motto at The FairyTale Farms is serving our customers with good experience on nature’s lap. It is

a great place to hang out with friends, have smashing birthdays’ or electrifying parties, enjoy pool-side candle light dinners while you toss great food in your mouth. It’s a place which serves all your needs. Apart from our hospitality and distinctive nature’s paradise we are also known for the great spirit of the place, Outdoor games and activities. It’s a place for light-hearted moments, enjoyment and celebrations. We make sure there is no dull moment in The FairyTale Farms.

ACTIVITIES

CRICKET PITCH

We have 15000 Sq.feet of Flood light Cricket Pitch where you can play all night with your buddies. This is the only place in Bangalore where you can play cricket and enjoy the stay with all other activities.

FOOTBALL TURF

This is the best place to play 6 or 4 a side football in nature’s lap.

SWIMMING POOL

Have a pool party all night with your family and friends.

FRISBEE DISC

Come on guys, you have 14000 Sq.feet of lush green lawns to enjoy Frisbee disc throws. It's going to be great fun!
